Safe Horizon is the nation’s leading victim assistance organization. Their mission is to prevent violence and promote justice for all by providing assistance, advocacy and support to victims who have experienced domestic violence, child abuse, sexual assault, stalking, human trafficking, youth homelessness and other crimes.

Visit https://www.safehorizon.org/ to make an online donation today or simply to learn more about Safe Horizon.
Girls Educational and Mentoring Services’ (GEMS) mission is to empower girls and young women, ages 12–24, who have experienced commercial sexual exploitation to exit the commercial sex industry and develop to their full potential. GEMS is committed to changing individual lives, transforming public perception, and revolutionizing the systems and policies that impact sexually exploited youth.

Commercial sexual exploitation is often linked to racism, poverty, gender-based violence, and the criminalization of youth. All of these factors are an integral part of any discussion, advocacy work or direct service programming that involves sexual exploitation. GEMS programs are based on the philosophy that each young woman deserves support and services to treat the trauma that she has experienced.

Visit http://www.gems-girls.org/ to make an online donation today or simply to learn more about GEMS.
The Association for Women's Rights in Development (AWID) is an international, feminist, membership organisation committed to achieving gender equality, sustainable development, and women’s human rights.

By supporting AWID, you can contribute to strengthening women’s rights movements and help drive transformative change for human rights, gender justice, and environmental sustainability worldwide.

Visit https://www.awid.org/ to make an online donation today or simply to learn more about AWID.
BronxWorks helps individuals and families improve their economic and social well-being. From toddlers to seniors, they feed, shelter, teach, and support Bronx locals to build a stronger community.

BronxWorks efforts include Food Stamp outreach, homelessness prevention, job skills training, budgeting and financial literacy counseling, benefits assistance, health insurance assistance, Early Childhood Learning Centers for preschoolers, a high school for students who have fallen behind academically, out-of-school youth programs, foster care prevention, and supportive housing among many other vital services.

Through generous donations such as yours, LIFT has helped nearly 100,000 community members and their families achieve their goals and get back onto solid ground.

LIFT provides community members with children under 8 years old assistance with searching for employment and housing, as well as accessing public benefits and childcare services. In addition, LIFT is a Single Stop provider offering access to SNAP applications and free legal, financial, and tax preparation services.

Visit http://www.liftcommunities.org/ to make an online donation today or simply to learn more about LIFT. If you have questions about donating, please contact Sang Hoon Lee, Chief Development Officer, at 267-253-0935 or by email at slee@liftcommunities.org.
