BOOM! provides care coordination, health navigation and nutrition education, counseling and food pantry services. They increase each client’s ability to access resources and services needed to improve their lives and achieve optimal health. They link clients to vital services, ensure that they have access and ongoing care by monitoring and case conferencing with all providers servicing the client. BOOM! works to empower, encourage, support, educate, advocate, refer, link and follow-up to ensure that each client receives the very best care and achieves the highest quality of life that he or she can.

POTS is a “one-stop shop” assisting individuals living in poverty on their journeys from crisis to stability and ultimately, self-sufficiency.

POTS provides food, clothing, shower, hair cuts, and a safe mailing address in addition to many other vital services such as assistance with public benefits (SNAP and health insurance applications on-site), supportive housing, eviction prevention, and general advocacy. POTS also provides referrals to detox and outpatient treatment, shelters, employment, financial counseling, educational programs and more.

POTS Family Club provides intensive case management, education, social and nutrition support to a select group of families. In addition to weekly meetings with a case manager, families gather once a week in the Community Dining Room. During this time, tutors provide homework assistance to children while their caregivers receive a lesson in nutrition and work with a professional chef to prepare a healthful and novel meal. At the end of each class, the children, caregivers and tutors come back together to share the meal. Highly-engaged families may also be eligible for holiday and special distributions. If you are interested in participating or would like more information, email the Family Club Coordinator at . familyclub@potsbronx.org

BronxWorks helps individuals and families improve their economic and social well-being. From toddlers to seniors, they feed, shelter, teach, and support Bronx locals to build a stronger community.

BronxWorks efforts include Food Stamp outreach, homelessness prevention, job skills training, budgeting and financial literacy counseling, benefits assistance, health insurance assistance, Early Childhood Learning Centers for preschoolers, a high school for students who have fallen behind academically, out-of-school youth programs, foster care prevention, and supportive housing among many other vital services.
